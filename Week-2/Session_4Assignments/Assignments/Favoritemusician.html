John Mayer

John Mayer is an American singer-songwriter and guitarist known for his soulful vocals and blues-inspired guitar playing. He was born on October 16, 1977, in Bridgeport, Connecticut, and began playing guitar at the age of 13. Mayer's music blends elements of rock, blues, pop, and folk, and he has won multiple Grammy Awards throughout his career.
